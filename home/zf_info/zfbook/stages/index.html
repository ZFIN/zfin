Larval Stages

  • Protruding-mouth (72h - 96h): 3.5mm total body length.
  • Day 4 (96h - 120h)
  • Day 5 (120h - 144h): 3.9mm total body length; 6 teeth.
  • Day 6 (144h - 168h)
  • Days 7-13 (7d - 13d): 4.5mm total body length, 8 teeth.
  • Days 14-20 (14d - 20d): 6.2mm total body length, 10 teeth.
  • Days 21-29 (21d - 29d): 7.8mm total body length, 10 teeth.

Juvenile Stages

  • Days 30-44 (30d - 44d): 10mm total body length, adult fins and pigment.
  • Days 45-89 (45d - 89d): 14mm total body length, 12 teeth.

Adult Stages

  • Adult (90d - 2y): Breeding adult.

Unknown Stage

  • Stage yet to be determined.
