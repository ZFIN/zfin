GENERAL METHODS FOR ZEBRAFISH CARE

Zebrafish Standard Protocol Form

(Source: University of Oregon)

The following PDF file (see note below) contains a template for animal use protocols involving zebrafish which has been approved by the veterinarian (OVSAC) and the animal use committee (IACUC) at the University of Oregon. It is provided to facilitate preparation of applications by users who must meet similar regulations. The text is also available on computer disk.

Download Protocol Form as PDF file (72k)

Note: Downloading PDF files
PDF files are readable by Adobe Acrobat Reader to yield a typeset-quality document far superior to any HTML version that could be made available directly on the WWW. However, you must first install Acrobat Reader on your machine and configure your browser to automatically load PDF files into it. Acrobat is free from the Adobe website and is easy to install (it comes with instructions).
