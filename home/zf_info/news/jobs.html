    (Center for Stem Cell and Regenerative Medicine at USC, Los Angeles, CA)


    A postdoctoral position is available in the lab of Dr. Gage Crump. The broad goal of the lab is to understand, at the molecular and cellular level, how organs acquire precise three-dimensional shapes. To this aim, we study the larval zebrafish head skeleton, where positional information and local epithelial signaling centers interact to shape a small set of unique cartilages.
