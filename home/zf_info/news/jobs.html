JOB DESCRIPTION: POSTDOC IN MOLECULAR GENETICS/DEVELOPMENTAL BIOLOGY AT THE MEDICAL FACULTY, UNIVERSITY OF TROMSØ, NORWAY

At the Medical Faculty, University of Tromsø, there is a post doc position open for 3 years in the field of molecular genetics/developmental biology. The position is a part of a collaboration between research groups at the Institute of Pharmacy and the Institute of Medical Biology, and it plays a central part in the establishment of zebrafish as a model system. The project is a part of the research strategy of the Medical Faculty.

To gain further information of the biological function of the gene products we study, their target genes and interaction partners, zebrafish will be used as a model-system. Knowledge about zebrafish/fish development (embryology, anatomy and physiology) and/or molecular genetic techniques such as immunohistochemistry and RNA in situ hybridisation is required.

To be considered for the position the candidate needs to have a PhD.
