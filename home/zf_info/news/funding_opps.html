Zebrafish Research Funding Opportunities

RFA on Genetic Studies of Obesity-Related Traits in Model Organisms -- posted 29 Nov 2004

The National Institute on Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases (NIDDK) is soliciting grant applications for genetic studies of obesity-related traits in zebrafish. The deadline for receipt of Letters of Intent is February 17, 2005, and the deadline for receipt of applications is March 17, 2005. The complete text of the Request for Applications is available at http://grants.nih.gov/grants/guide/rfa-files/RFA-DK-03-018.html
