Developmental Biology Symposium

September 27-28, 2004
University of Minnesota - Minneapolis

13th Annual University of Minnesota Developmental Biology Symposium
From Egg to Organ: Development and Disease
September 27-28, 2004

This year's Symposium will focus on development and disease. We will consider how pathogenic conditions can arise as a result of aberrant developmental mechanisms. Presentations will also explore how model organisms and genome projects further our understanding of the molecular mechanisms that underlie disease processes.

For registration information and conference details visit www.med.umn.edu/dbc or contact Tami Jauert at 612-624-4981.

Fish Genetics and Development Workshop - Chinese Academy of Sciences

October 11-14, 2004
Wuhan,China

The overseas Chinese biologists are invited to present their latest progress in fish genetics and development studies. The workshop will include topics such as fish development, genomics, transgenesis and technology, endocrinology, and immunology. During the workshop, Drs. Perry Hackett, Choy Leong Hew, Yoshitaka Nagahama, Bernard Thisse and Monte Westerfield will give special lectures.

Please check out the websites http://www.ihb.ac.cn/fishdev/index.htm or http://clonfish.myrice.com for guidelines of abstract submission, workshop registration, scientific program, and other related information. (better use a PC browser to view these websites).

Contacting person: Dr. Yong-Hua Sun (Institute of Hydrobiology, Chinese Academy of Sciences)

15th International Society of Developmental Biologists Congress

September 3-7, 2005
Sydney, Australia

HOSTED BY:
The International Society of Developmental Biologists (ISDB).

ORGANISING COMMITTEE:
Richard Harvey (Chair), Sally Dunwoodie (Co-chair), Peter Currie (Program Chair)

VENUE:
The Congress will be held at Sydney's famous Convention & Exhibition Centre on Darling Harbour. The Sydney Convention and Exhibition Centre is the largest and most technologically advanced facility in Australia. It has been purpose built to host a spectrum of conferences, international congresses and exhibitions. It is only five minutes from Sydney's city centre and Opera House, and within walking distance of many restaurants and shops.

TRADE EXHIBITION AND SPONSORSHIP:
A trade exhibition will be held in conjunction with the Congress. The exhibition will cover a wide range of products and services in the field of developmental biology. Companies wishing to participate as a sponsor or promote their products/services within the trade exhibition are encouraged to contact the Congress Managers for details, or submit your interest online.

CALL FOR ABSTRACTS:
Submission of abstracts for presentation during the Congress will be available online from Mid 2004.

EXPRESSION OF INTEREST: If you are interested in attending this Congress and would like further information, please visit the congress website and complete the online form. A registration form will be sent to you when they become available.
