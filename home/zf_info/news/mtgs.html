2nd TLL Life Sciences Symposium: Genomics in Cell and Developmental Biology

January 29-30, 2004
Temasek Life Sciences Laboratory, Singapore

Abstract Deadline: 20 December 2003

Organized by Temasek Life Sciences Laboratory (TLL), Singapore

TLL symposia are annual low-cost meetings held at the Temasek Life Sciences Laboratory, Singapore. The upcoming symposium (29 and 30 January 2004) aims to highlight research that primarily utilizes genomic approaches to study some outstanding questions in the areas of cell and developmental biology using various model organisms. It will be held at Temasek Life Sciences Laboratory, located within the campus of the National University of Singapore. The symposium will begin in the morning of Thursday, 29 January 2004 and will conclude with dinner on Friday, 30 January 2004. The program will consist of both oral and poster presentations.

We strongly encourage postdocs and students to submit abstracts for poster presentations. A number of abstracts will be selected for short oral presentations. The deadline for abstract submission is 20 December 2003.
