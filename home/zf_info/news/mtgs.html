2002 Australia/New Zealand Zebrafish Workshop

February 14, 2002, Melbourne, Australia
Back to top

NOW IS THE TIME TO REGISTER

The 2002 Zebrafish Workshop will be held in Melbourne on Thursday 14th February, 2002. The full day session will start with registration (8.30-9.00 a.m.) and continue through the day with an evening mystery event (it remains a mystery, even to the Organising Committee). We also plan an optional focus session in the morning of Friday 15th February on "Positional Cloning", based on projects in progress, for those who are interested.

The invited guest speaker will be Robert Geisler, from Tübingen, Germany. Robert is a key player in the Zebrafish Genome Project. He has recently confirmed his attendance. He will also be participating in the Lorne Genome Conference the following week. The program aims to showcase the full variety of zebrafish research in Australia. Presentations by registrants will be of varied lengths -you will notified of your time allocation by 1/Feb/2002. In this forum, presentations of projects in their early stages and of projects in progress are appropriate.

NOTE:

1.  It is a THURSDAY not a Wednesday.

2.  There is an optional FRIDAY session on positional cloning.

3.  We will endeavour to give everyone a presentation slot who wants one.

4.  The registration fee ($100 + $10 GST) will cover catering over the 1.5 days and the cost of the evening event. Registration and payments are requested in advance for accounting reasons.

5.  This year we will give presenters the option of submitting an abstract for distribution at the meeting if they want to. We will duplicate and collate those abstracts that are submitted after notification that a presentation is scheduled (abstract deadline Wed/6/Feb/02). The submission of abstracts is optional and at the discretion of the presenter.

6.  The venue and equipment will handle Powerpoint presentations as well as other media like photographic slides. Our computer whizzes will be on-hand to address any Mac/PC or other technical issues that might arise on the spot.

REGISTRATION PROCEDURES:

1.  Register now by completing the attached form.

  • it is necessary to return the form by post with your advance payment
  • if you are wanting to give a presentation, we would greatly appreciate receiving a fax or e-mail of your completed registration form in advance of your posted form.

The DEADLINE for registration if you are wanting to give a presentation is 28/January 2002. The DEADLINE for general registration is 4/February/2002.

2.  If you are given a presentation slot, you will be notified by email by Friday 1/February of the details, time allocation etc. You will be given the option of e-mailing an abstract (fitting onto an A4 page) by Wed/6/February.

3.  If you have a positional cloning project for workshopping in the Friday session, please contact me directly so that we can plan with you how to best incorporate it into the session.

ADVERTISING

Our advertising relies on the "Word O'Mouth" & "Personal Recommendation" freeware. Notification is being sent to every participant of the 2001 Workshop. Please pass it on to anyone else you think may be interested in attending this year.
