Job Openings


ZFIN, the zebrafish model organism database, seeks scientific curators to join our dynamic, interactive team of biologists and computer scientists at the University of Oregon in Eugene, OR. .

Scientific Curators
ZFIN curators acquire, evaluate and analyze information about zebrafish gene structures, expression patterns, and mutant phenotypes. They integrate information from disparate sources and interact with research laboratories to facilitate electronic submission of data. Curators also participate in database and interface design by providing biological perspectives for new database content and displays. Applicants should have a Ph.D. or M.S. with appropriate experience in molecular biology or genetics is required. Applicants should have strong backgrounds in developmental genetics, preferably in zebrafish.

More information is available on ZFIN about the staff and the database project.
