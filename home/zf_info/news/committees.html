Committees and Working Groups

Zebrafish Nomenclature Committee (ZNC)

The Zebrafish Nomenclature Committee, appointed at a Cold Spring Harbor meeting several years ago, provides guidelines and advice for naming genes and mutants. These conventions are based on input from the zebrafish community and on the rules used for naming human and mouse genes.
