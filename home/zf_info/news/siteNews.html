ZFIN Site News & Status

This page contains news about the Zebrafish Information Network web site (zfin.org). This includes scheduled down time, site upgrades, and new features. If you have any questions or comments, please contact Pat Edwards.

Power outage from 2001/09/08 to 2001/09/09

2001/09/04: Due to a scheduled power outage on the University of Oregon campus, the main ZFIN site (http://zfin.org) and the backup ZFIN site (http://backup.zfin.org) will not be available from late Saturday through late Sunday, Pacific Daylight Time in the US (which is early Sunday through early Monday, GMT).

However, the mirror sites at Melbourne,Australia, Mishima, Japan, and Strasbourg, France will be available during this time.

Merged map (ZMAP) available

2001/06/22: The option of viewing several maps 'merged' onto one backbone is now available.

Support for Internet Explorer added to ZFIN

2001/05/16: You can now access ZFIN using Internet Explorer. Version 5.0 or higher is required on either Macintosh and PC platforms.

Main site was down on 2001/03/30

2001/04/02: The main site (zfin.org) was down on 2001/03/30 for several hours for maintenance.

2001/02/10: The main site (zfin.org) was down on 2001/02/10 for a few hours for maintenance.
