ZFIN Site News & Status

This page contains news about the Zebrafish Information Network web site (zfin.org). This includes scheduled down time, site upgrades, and new features. If you have any questions or comments, please contact Sherry Giglia.


Power outage at ZFIN, 2004/03/23

On Tuesday, March 23, ZFIN will be down from 10:00 am to 12:00 pm PST (6:00 pm to 8:00 pm GMT) to complete wiring for our new uninterruptable power supply (UPS).

Power outage at ZFIN, 2004/03/03

On Wednesday, March 3, ZFIN will be down from 10:30 am to 12:00 pm PST (6:30 pm to 8:00 pm GMT) to do wiring for our new uninterruptable power supply (UPS).

Network problems at ZFIN, 2004/02

Starting on 2004/02/11 and intermittently for about 10 days after that, ZFIN experienced severe network problems. These were due to a number of problems, including problems with local network switches and larger problems with the University of Oregon network.

Power outage at ZFIN, 2004/01/21

The power was out at ZFIN for just under an hour. This was to install wiring to support ZFIN's new uninterruptable power supply (UPS).

Gene Ontology(GO) annotations in ZFIN, 2002/09/18

GO terms are now available on ZFIN's revised gene page.

Dawid Lab expression patterns in ZFIN, 2002/09/18

In situ patterns from the Dawid Lab gene expression screen may be viewed with the ZFIN expression query form.

Power Outage - ZFIN unavailable for part of 2002/09/22

Power will be out on the University of Oregon campus on Sunday, September 22 from 07:30 am and 09:30 am PDT (03:30 pm to 05:30 am GMT). ZFIN will be unavailable during this time.
