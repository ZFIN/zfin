ZFIN Site News & Status

This page contains news about the Zebrafish Information Network web site (zfin.org). This includes scheduled down time, site upgrades, and new features. If you have any questions or comments, please contact Pat Edwards.

Recent Power outages at ZFIN

2001/09/19: ZFIN has been offline several times during the past 10 days. This been due, directly and indirectly, to several power outages on the University of Oregon campus.

There was a scheduled power outage from 2001/09/08 through 2001/09/09, when the University of Oregon was doing equipment testing and upgrades. This power outage went according to plan. However, when we tried to bring up the ZFIN production server after the outage, the server suffered multiple disk failures. It took more than a day to bring up ZFIN on our backup server. By midweek, the problems with the production server had been fixed, and ZFIN was again, briefly down while we switched machines.

The following weekend (2001/09/14) there was a large electrical storm (a rare thing in Eugene) that knocked out power again. ZFIN was again down for several hours.

We are in the process of replacing our disk systems. ZFIN may experience brief outages in the coming weeks as we install the new drives and transfer data to them.

Merged map (ZMAP) available

2001/06/22: The option of viewing several maps 'merged' onto one backbone is now available.

Support for Internet Explorer added to ZFIN

2001/05/16: You can now access ZFIN using Internet Explorer. Version 5.0 or higher is required on either Macintosh and PC platforms.
