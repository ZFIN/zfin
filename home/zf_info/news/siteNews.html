ZFIN Site News & Status

Last updated 2001/03/23

This page contains news about the Zebrafish Information Network web site (zfin.org). This includes scheduled down time and site upgrades. If you have any questions or comments, please contact Pat Edwards.

Main site will be down on 2001/03/30

2001/03/23: The main site (zfin.org) will down for several hours on 2001/03/30 for maintenance. During this time ZFIN will be available at the backup ZFIN server:

http://backup.zfin.org

Maintenance is scheduled to start at 8 pm GMT (noon Pacific Time in the US) and is expected to last from 4 to 6 hours.

The mirror sites (Melbourne,Australia, Mishima, Japan, and Strasbourg, France) will also be up during this time.

Main site was down on 2001/02/10

2001/02/10: The main site (zfin.org) was down on 2001/02/10 for a few hours for maintenance.
