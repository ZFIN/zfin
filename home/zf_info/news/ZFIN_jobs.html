Job Opening

Java Architect
ZFIN, the zebrafish model organism database, located at the University of Oregon is looking for a highly motivated Java Architect to work with a team of biologists and computer scientists to design, develop and maintain a dynamic bioinformatics database infrastructure for integration, consolidation and interpretation of zebrafish gene structures, expression patterns and mutant phenotypes.

The ZFIN Java Architect is a multi-disciplinary technical role that requires strong hands-on experience with object-oriented design and implementation, servlet containers, applications servers, object-relational mappings, SQL/stored procedural language development, relational database management systems and version control software. The successful candidate will also have experience establishing multiple development environments, knowledge of Web application security and demonstrated team leadership.

Minimum qualifications: Bachelor's degree with 5+ years experience in Java architecture and programming, Master's preferred. Knowledge of biology is a plus. Send resume with names and phone numbers of 3 references to:
