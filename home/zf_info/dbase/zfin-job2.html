Job Opening
Data Editor for Genetic Database


Join an Exciting Research Project on an International Biological Database

Job Description: The zebrafish database research group at the University of Oregon seeks a person to help edit, enter, and update molecular, genetic and developmental information maintained in an on-line database. The job will include:
  • On-line and library searches for information from published journals
  • Cataloging genes, mutations and genetic map information
  • Communication with researchers world-wide to obtain up-to-date information
Requirements: Applicants should have solid training in biology with particular emphasis in genetics and gene cloning. Experience reading original research articles and conducting library catalog and on-line reference searches also necessary. Computer and database skills desirable but not required. Recent graduates encouraged to apply.
  • Degree in biology or equivalent
  • Experience with genetics and molecular biology
  • Strong writing and communication skills
