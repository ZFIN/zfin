Other Genome Resources

Fish Specific Databases

  • Fishbase A global information system on fishes.
  • Fugu Fugu genome mapping project.
  • Medakafish Online information service for medaka ricefish
  • Pufferfish:Tetraodon nigroviridis Genome Analysis of Tetraodon.
  • Stickleback Stickelback data at the Stanford Genome Evolution Center
  • Tilapia Tilapia genome project.
  • Xiphophorus Information on the Poeciliid fish Xiphophorus.
  • Zebrafish Information Network (ZFIN) Online information service for Danio rerio

Other Organism Databases

  • Genomic Information for Model Eukaryotic Organisms Database
  • C. elegans The WormBase project.
  • Cattle Institut National de Recherche Agronomique, Jouy-en-Josas, Laboratoire de genetique biochimique, France. Loci database; contacts database; homology queries; linkage maps.
  • Cattle / Pig / Sheep USDA Meat Animal Research Center. Clickable chromosome maps; loci database.
  • Chimpanzee Chimpanzee genome.
  • Comparative Cattle / Chicken / Pig / Sheep Roslin Institute, Scotland. Graphical linkage and physical maps for Sheep, Human, Pig, Cattle, Chicken; comparative maps for these species generated on the fly; loci database; reference database.
  • Dog University of California, Berkeley, CA, USA. Genetic disorder information; marker information; dog genome project information.
  • Drosophila Drosophila Mapping database (FlyBase) Harvard University, Cambridge, MA, USA. Drosophila citations; researchers addresses; loci and allele information; chromosomal aberrations; genetic map information; gene products; stock center and private lab Drosophila stocks; nucleic and protein sequence accession numbers; genomic clones; Drosophila Genome Project data; Drosophila images.
  • Goat Goatmap.
  • Human A Gene Map of the Human Genome: A searchable map giving the location of human expressed sequence tags (ESTs).
  • Human: Online Mendelian Inheritance in Man (OMIM) A searchable database providing phenotypic and molecular summaries of phenotypes and genes, with links to abstracts in MedLine, genetic map positions, and protein and nucleic acid sequences.
  • Human: The Genome DataBase Largely overlaps OMIM, but does not provide phenotypic descriptions.
  • Human: The HuGeMap database Links to genetic maps at Genethon and physical maps from CEPH and Whitehead Institute.
  • Kangaroo Genome Project
  • Mouse Mouse Genome Database (MGD), The Jackson Laboratory, Bar Harbor, ME, USA. Genetic maps of various data sets with homologies; comparative mapping data (>50 mammalian species); Oxford grids; experimental mapping results; genetic markers/mutants/ chromsome aberration data; molecular probes/PCR primer characterization; polymorphisms and alleles for strains and mutants; phenotypic descriptions of genes/mutants; inbred strain characteristics; references, interactive and postscript map display tools.
  • Mouse UK MRC Human Genome Mapping Project Resource Centre, Cambridge, UK.
  • Mouse Whole Mouse Catalog, Caltech, Pasadena, CA, USA.. A guide to WWW sites with information about mice and rats.
  • Pig, horse, cattle, pig & chicken US Livestock Genome Mapping Projects. Information on pig , sheep, horse, cattle and chicken mapping programs.
  • Rat Rat Genome Database
  • Rat RATMAP database. Tabular genome mapping data.
  • Sheep UK Sheep Genome Mapping Project.
  • Xenopus Xenopus Web resource.
