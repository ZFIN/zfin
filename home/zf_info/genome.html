Related Genome Databases

  • Genomic Information for Model Eukaryotic Organisms Database
  • C. elegans = The WormBase project.
  • Cattle = Institut National de Recherche Agronomique, Jouy-en-Josas, Laboratoire de genetique biochimique, France. Loci database; contacts database; homology queries; linkage maps.
  • Cattle / Pig / Sheep = USDA Meat Animal Research Center. Clickable chromosome maps; loci database.
  • Cattle / Horse / Pig / Mouse = Agriculture, Forestry, and Fisheries Research Council, Japan. Clickable chromosome maps; loci database.
  • Comparative Cattle / Chicken / Pig / Sheep = Roslin Institute, Scotland. Graphical linkage and physical maps for Sheep, Human, Pig, Cattle, Chicken; comparative maps for these species generated on the fly; loci database; reference database.
  • Comparative = Comparative Mapping Home Page, La Trobe University, Australia. HUGO Comparative Committee reports; graphical comparative mapping database interface; comparative mapping figures and tables; links to related sites.
  • Dog = University of California, Berkeley, CA, USA. Genetic disorder information; marker information; dog genome project information.
  • Drosophila = Drosophila Mapping database (FlyBase) Harvard University, Cambridge, MA, USA. Drosophila citations; researchers addresses; loci and allele information; chromosomal aberrations; genetic map information; gene products; stock center and private lab Drosophila stocks; nucleic and protein sequence accession numbers; genomic clones; Drosophila Genome Project data; Drosophila images.
  • Fugu = Fugu genome mapping project.
  • Human = A Gene Map of the Human Genome: A searchable map giving the location of human expressed sequence tags (ESTs).
  • Human: Online Mendelian Inheritance in Man (OMIM) = A searchable database providing phenotypic and molecular summaries of phenotypes and genes, with links to abstracts in MedLine, genetic map positions, and protein and nucleic acid sequences.
  • Human: The Genome DataBase = Largely overlaps OMIM, but does not provide phenotypic descriptions.
  • Human: The HuGeMap database = Links to genetic maps at Genethon and physical maps from CEPH and Whitehead Institute.
  • Marsupials / Monotremes = The Marsupial Genome Database, La Trobe University, Australia. Tabular genome mapping data.
  • Mouse = Mouse Genome Database (MGD), The Jackson Laboratory, Bar Harbor, ME, USA. Email contact: mgi-help@informatics.jax.org. Genetic maps of various data sets with homologies; comparative mapping data (>50 mammalian species); Oxford grids; experimental mapping results; genetic markers/mutants/ chromsome aberration data; molecular probes/PCR primer characterization; polymorphisms and alleles for strains and mutants; phenotypic descriptions of genes/mutants; inbred strain characteristics; references, interactive and postscript map display tools.
  • Mouse = UK mirror
  • Mouse = UK MRC Human Genome Mapping Project Resource Centre, Cambridge, UK. Email contact: support@hgmp.mrc.ac.uk
  • Mouse = Mouse Genome Mammalian Genetics Unit, Harwell, Oxon, UK. Biology resources; Mousedb, an ACeDB display of maps, chromosome anomalies and human-mouse homologies.
  • Mouse = Whole Mouse Catalog, Caltech, Pasadena, CA, USA. Email contact: mercer@caltech.edu. A guide to WWW sites with information about mice and rats.
  • Pig & chicken = US Livestock Genome Mapping Projects. Information on pig and chicken mapping programs.
  • Rat = RATMAP database. Tabular genome mapping data. Database under development for this site.
  • Zebrafish = Institute of Neuroscience, University of Oregon, Eugene, OR, USA. Email contact: edwards@uoneuro.uoregon.edu. Strains; probes; researcher contact information; linkage map; database development information; methods; complete zebrafish publication list.
