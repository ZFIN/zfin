Zebrafish deficiency strains

Deficiencies are currently being generated and catalogued as part of the Zebrafish Genome Iniative. The funded project (Marnie Halpern and Andreas Fritz, co-PIs) has 4 specific aims:

1. To collect, preserve and catalogue existing deficiency and translocation strains.
2. To recover and characterize new gamma-ray induced deficiencies.
3. To localize and determine the extent of deficiencies and assemble a genomic DNA deletion panel uncovering the zebrafish genome.
4. To document haploid and diploid deficiency phenotypes for the eventual correlation of expressed sequences with mutant phenotypes.

Complete information about these lines is currently available on a webserver at the Carnegie Institution of Washington: Data are also available in a searchable form with links to information about missing genes, markers, map locations, etc. on ZFIN.
