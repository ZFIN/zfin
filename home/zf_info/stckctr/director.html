Job Opening: Director of Zebrafish International Resource Center


The Zebrafish International Resource Center on the University of Oregon campus in Eugene seeks a Director. The Resource Center is an NIH funded Biomedical Models Research Center. Primary activities include:

1) Acquisition, maintenance and distribution of zebrafish genetic stocks and research materials.

2) Consultation and pathology services, directed by a staff veterinarian and a fish pathologist, including characterization of endemic diseases and development of diagnostic and control methods.

3) Development of improved husbandry and cryopreservation methods.

The Director will be responsible for all operations of the Resource Center and will need to interact effectively with the research community. Candidates should have a background in genetics (preferably zebrafish) and strong organizational and management skills. PhD required. Starting salary $60,000-$80,000 depending upon experience. In addition to the Director, the Resource Center will recruit 4-5 new staff members during the coming year.
