Zebrafish International Resource Center
Request Form

Although the Resource Center is still functioning on a limited basis as we move into our new building, we will do the best we can to help you obtain the materials you need. You can get most of the information you need on-line:
  • Types of lines: http://zfin.org/ZFIN
  • Availability of lines: http://zfin.org/zf_info/stckctr/guide.html
  • Obtaining lines: http://zfin.org/zf_info/stckctr/guide.html
  • Fees: http://zfin.org/zf_info/stckctr/fees.html
  • Resource Center Information: http://zfin.org/zf_info/stckctr/stckctr.html

If you are requesting animals, we ask that the Principle Investigator verify that they will be treated according to NIH animal use guidelines. We will fax you the necessary form.
Please feel free to communicate with us if you have any questions: fish_requests@zfin.org

Acknowledging materials and services:
The Zebrafish International Resource Center is supported by grant #RR12546 from the NIH-NCRR. Please acknowledge the Zebrafish International Resource Center and this grant number in all publications resulting from the materials and/or services we are providing to you.
Thank you.
