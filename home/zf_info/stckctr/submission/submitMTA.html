Zebrafish License Agreement
 
Fish covered by an MTA can be submitted to the ZIRC only after you complete the Zebrafish License Agreement. In short, the Agreement states: ZIRC is not to distribute the fish to any for-profit third party without prior consent of the provider, ZIRC agrees to ask whether the purchaser is a for-profit entity, and if so identified, advise the purchaser that it must first obtain a use license from PROVIDER. PROVIDER shall notify ZIRC of its written consent within (10) days of execution of any use license for FISH. It shall be the sole responsibility of PROVIDER to obtain and enforce any use licenses. Also, ZIRC will inform all recipients that the FISH and their descendants, if any, shall not be bred or used for sale nor redistributed to any third party outside of the recipient’s institution, and shall be used solely for research purposes.
