AO as a Facet

  • Only directly annotated terms are selected from the database
  • High level organizational terms are excluded as stopwords
  • Parent terms are inserted into the index using a solr synonym file
    • brain stored as: brain, nervous system, central nervous system, ...
  • Child terms are expanded at query time, so that they can be highlighted
    • brain expands to: (brain OR midbrain OR hindbrain OR forebrain ... )
