Search Tips
  1. Construct: Enter one or more terms. Terms can include part or all of a construct name. Your search terms will be compared to transgenic constructs and previous names.
  2. Promoter:Searches constructs in which a promoter, or regulatory region, has been designated.
  3. Coding Sequence:Searches constructs in which a coding sequence, or expressed gene, has been designated
  4. Inserted in Gene:Searches constructs that have been shown to be inserted in a specific gene
  5. Reporter Expression in:Typing in this field will auto-complete with suggested terms from the Zebrafish Anatomical Ontology. The search will find constructs that have reporter expression annotated to the term entered.
  6. All Constructs:This is checked by default and searches against all transgenic constructs, including enhancer trap, gene trap and promoter trap constructs.
  7. Transgenic Construct:Limits your search to transgenic constructs.
  8. Enhancer Trap Construct:Limits your search to constructs that are enhancer trap constructs.
  9. Gene Trap Construct:Limits your search to constructs that are gene trap constructs
  10. Promoter Trap Construct:Limits your search to constructs that are promoter trap constructs.
